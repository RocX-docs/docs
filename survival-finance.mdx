# 🌍 Survival Finance

## Finance that remembers participation before capital

Finance is used to

measuring capital.

But human activity,

trust,

and contributions built over time

are rarely remembered.

Survival Finance begins

in that empty space.

---

> Capital moves.
>
> Participation compounds.
>
> Trust endures.

## 📖 Survival Finance is

not finance built

to gather more capital.

It is finance built

to give more opportunity

to those who participate longer.

We want to create

a new era

where participation becomes

one of the most important forms

of financial data.
