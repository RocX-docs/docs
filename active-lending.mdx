## What Active Lending is

Active Lending is RocX's lending experience that extends beyond deposit, borrow, and repay.

It connects lending activity to participation, Proof of Activity, Active Energy, and Reputation so the user's financial behavior can become part of a larger record.

<Info>
Active Lending makes post-lending activity visible. Repayment, continued participation, and responsible activity can all become signals inside RocX.
</Info>

## Why lending needs a longer memory

In traditional lending, the relationship often ends when the transaction ends.

The user deposits, borrows, repays, and leaves. The protocol may record the transaction, but the broader experience rarely becomes useful context for the next opportunity.

RocX changes that path.

Financial activity can become participation. Participation can become reputation. Reputation can support better access over time.

## The RocX perspective

RocX does not treat lending itself as the main innovation.

The important shift is what happens after lending: activity continues, records grow, and the relationship can become stronger over time.

Good finance should give users a reason to return.
