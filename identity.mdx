# 🪪 Identity

## You are not defined

by your wallet.

You are defined

by your activity.

In Web3,

anyone can create

a new wallet.

But trust

cannot be created

from nothing.

RocX builds user Identity

through activity.

---

<Info>
📘 **Definition**

Identity is

an on-chain profile

built from participation,

contribution,

reputation,

and trust.

Identity is not created once.

It grows

with activity.
</Info>

> Wallets identify accounts.
>
> Activity identifies people.

## 💡 Why does Identity matter?

Most of Web3 today

remembers wallets.

But we want to remember

the person behind the wallet.

How long they participated,

how much they contributed,

and how much trust they built

should live inside Identity.
