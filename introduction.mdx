# 📖 Why Are We Still Here?

## Why are we still here?

Crypto has collapsed

again and again.

New projects appear,

then disappear.

And still,

people keep coming back.

Why?

---

> People don't stay because everything succeeds.
>
> They stay because they still believe.

## We do not think

people remain in crypto

only to make money.

New technology,

new finance,

new possibilities.

And the belief

that one day,

a better system can be built.
