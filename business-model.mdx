Traditional finance often creates revenue from transactions.

Many DeFi protocols create revenue from liquidity.

RocX starts from a different place: continuous participation.

When users keep returning, financial activity becomes healthier. When financial activity becomes healthier, revenue can grow in a more sustainable way.

> Revenue is an outcome.
>
> Participation is the engine.

---

## What the RocX business model is

RocX is designed around a growth model where user activity strengthens the ecosystem.

Participation creates retention. Retention creates healthier financial activity. That activity can generate revenue and support further ecosystem growth.

<Info>
RocX revenue is designed to grow with user participation, not by extracting value from users who leave after one transaction.
</Info>

## The RocX perspective

RocX does not aim to push more transactions for their own sake.

It aims to create a reason for users to keep participating.

Continuous participation creates healthy financial activity. Healthy financial activity creates a more sustainable business.
