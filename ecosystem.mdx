RocX is built as a financial journey, not a one-way transaction.

A user enters the ecosystem, takes action, proves that activity, and builds a record that can lead to more opportunity.

Each step creates the next.

> Every journey begins with participation.

---

## The RocX journey

The RocX ecosystem is designed around a repeating cycle.

Deposit starts the journey. Exploration creates activity. Proof turns that activity into a record. Survival means that the record keeps growing into reputation, identity, and future access.

<Info>
RocX connects participation to Active Energy, Reputation, Identity, and new financial opportunity.
</Info>

</Tip>

## The RocX perspective

RocX is not trying to create a single financial action.

It is building a journey where activity leads to proof, proof leads to reputation, and reputation leads to more participation.

<Note>
RocX turns participation into a cycle.

That cycle is what makes Survival Finance work.
</Note>
