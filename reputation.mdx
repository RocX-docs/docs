# 🧠 Reputation Layer

## Trust becomes an asset when it is recorded.

Capital

can move at any time.

But trust

is built over time.

RocX

remembers and accumulates

that trust on-chain.

---

<Info>
📘 **Definition**

The Reputation Layer

is RocX's core infrastructure

for continuously accumulating

user activity and contribution,

then connecting trust

to financial data.

Reputation is not a score.

It is a record of trust

that creates more opportunity.
</Info>

> Trust should not disappear.
>
> It should compound.

## 💡 Why does reputation matter?

Most finance today

remembers assets,

but not people.

It cannot distinguish

someone who participated for years

from someone who just arrived.

We believe

trust should compound too.
