# How reputation becomes opportunity

Capital can move quickly.

Trust takes time to build.

RocX records that trust on-chain so it can grow into reputation and support better financial access.

> Trust should not disappear.
>
> It should compound.

---

## What the Reputation Layer is

The Reputation Layer is RocX's infrastructure for turning verified participation into a record of trust.

It connects user activity, Active Energy, and Proof of Activity to a reputation profile that can be used across the ecosystem.

<Info>
Reputation is recorded trust. It helps RocX recognize users by how they participate, not only by what they hold.
</Info>

## Why reputation matters

Most financial systems remember assets better than people.

They can see balances, collateral, and transactions, but they often miss the difference between a user who has contributed for years and a user who arrived today.

RocX treats that difference as important.

Participation over time should become a signal of trust. Trust should become a basis for opportunity.

---

## Traditional credit and on-chain reputation

| Traditional Finance | RocX |
| --- | --- |
| Credit score | Reputation |
| Financial history | Activity history |
| Assets and income | Participation and contribution |
| Documents and institutions | Proof of Activity |
| Off-chain approval | On-chain trust record |

<Tip>
On-chain reputation does not replace credit. It adds a new layer of context for users whose activity and contribution deserve to be recognized.
</Tip>

## The RocX perspective

RocX believes future finance will evaluate more than what a user holds.

It will also consider how consistently they participate, what they contribute, and how much trust they have earned.

The Reputation Layer is the foundation for that future.
