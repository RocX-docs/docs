## Your activity defines you

> Who are you on-chain?

Today, most wallets are defined by the assets they hold. The size of their capital, the tokens they trade, the NFTs they collect.

But assets do not tell your story. Assets cannot explain how long you stayed, how actively you participated, or how much trust you earned.

RocX believes identity should be built through action. Your activity should define you.

Every deposit, every mission, every achievement, every contribution. These actions become part of your on-chain identity.

In Web3, a wallet holds assets. But identity holds a story.

RocX introduces a continuous identity layer that grows with the user.

A continuous identity layer that grows with the user. This identity can include:

Identity is not something you buy. It is something you build.

Over time, your identity grows stronger, your reputation grows deeper, and new opportunities begin to appear.

In Survival Finance, what matters most is not what you own. It is who you become.
