## A new financial layer

Traditional finance rewards capital.

**Survival Finance rewards participation.**

Traditional finance is built around money. DeFi is built around liquidity. But neither fully recognizes the people who keep participating.

| Financial paradigm | What it is built around |
| --- | --- |
| Traditional finance | Money (capital) |
| DeFi | Liquidity |
| Survival Finance | Participation |

The finance of the future does not depend simply on how much capital you own. It depends on how long you stay, how actively you contribute, and how much trust you build.

We call this new paradigm **Survival Finance**.

Survival Finance is a financial layer where participation becomes measurable, activity is treated as valuable, and reputation creates opportunity. It is designed for people who:

- keep exploring.
- stay active over time.
- contribute to the ecosystem.
- build trust through their actions.

Survival Finance does not replace DeFi. It evolves DeFi.

Capital still matters. But capital alone is not enough.

Activity matters. Reputation matters. Commitment matters.

Commitment matters.

The financial system of the future must reward not only wealth but also participation. That future begins right here.
