## Deposit · Explore · Prove · Survive

RocX is more than just a lending protocol. RocX is an ecosystem built around participation.

Every user follows a simple but meaningful journey.

**Deposit. Explore. Prove. Survive.** These four actions form the foundation of Survival Finance.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Deposit" icon="vault">
    Every journey begins with commitment.

    By depositing assets, users not only earn returns but also become part of the RocX ecosystem.

    Capital matters. Capital creates opportunity. But capital alone cannot define value.

    Deposit is only the beginning, not the destination.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Explore" icon="compass">
    Finance should not be passive.

    Users explore missions, take part in community activities, discover new opportunities, and interact with the ecosystem.

    Exploration creates experience. Experience creates activity. And activity creates value.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Prove" icon="fingerprint">
    Every meaningful action leaves a trace.

    Participation becomes measurable. Consistency becomes visible. Commitment becomes proof.

    At RocX, users do not simply own assets. They build history.

    Every action contributes to their on-chain identity and strengthens their Proof of Activity.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Survive" icon="shield">
    Markets change. Narratives change. Opportunities come and go.

    But those who keep participating, exploring, and contributing create something far more valuable. Trust.

    Survival is not luck. It is the result of continued participation.

    At RocX, those who stay gain more opportunity. Because Survival Finance was made for those who stay.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Deposit. Explore. Prove. Survive.

<Note>
This is not just the RocX ecosystem. It is a new way to experience finance.
</Note>
