# RocX vs Traditional DeFi

## A New Value Flywheel

Traditional DeFi

succeeded at growing capital.

RocX builds on that foundation

and proposes

a new financial structure

where participation and trust

grow together.

---

<Info>
📘 **Definition**

RocX does not aim

to replace

the TVL-centered growth model.

It builds a new Value Flywheel

on top of it,

where Participation,

Reputation,

and Retention

continue to accumulate.
</Info>

> Capital starts a protocol.
>
> Participation sustains it.
