# Proof of Activity

## When activity is recorded, it becomes finance.

Traditional finance

records the movement of money.

RocX

records human activity.

Proof of Activity

turns participation

into measurable financial data.

---

<Info>
📘 **Definition**

Proof of Activity(PoA) is

RocX's core mechanism

for measuring,

verifying,

and accumulating on-chain activity

as sustainable financial data.

PoA is not just an activity log.

It is a new financial standard

for creating future opportunity and trust.
</Info>

> Every meaningful action deserves to be remembered.

## 💡 Why prove activity?

In most DeFi systems today,

when activity ends,

the value of that record disappears with it.

But we believe

activity should accumulate

like an asset.

Activity creates reputation,

and reputation creates

more opportunity.
