## Why are we still here?

The world of finance has advanced rapidly. Trades have become faster, markets more efficient, and access easier than ever before.

But one question remains.

> Why do people still struggle to stay?

In Web3, capital moves freely, liquidity flows without end, and protocols compete to attract deposits. Yet users rarely stay. Relationships fade, and with every new cycle people have to start over from the beginning.

We think something important is missing. Not capital, not liquidity, not incentives.

**It is participation.**

Those who keep exploring, keep contributing, stay through uncertainty, and believe in the future deserve to be recognized. RocX begins from this belief.

We are building a new financial layer. A place where activity becomes value, reputation becomes opportunity, and survival is rewarded.

<Note>
We call this **Survival Finance**.
</Note>
