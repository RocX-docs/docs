## The next category of finance

Every generation of finance creates a new category.

| Generation of finance | The category it created |
| --- | --- |
| Traditional finance | Banking |
| DeFi | Open finance |
| RocX | Survival Finance |

Traditional finance created banking, and DeFi created open finance. And now, a new category is beginning to emerge. We call it **Survival Finance**.

Financial markets have become more efficient, more accessible, and more open.

But one challenge still remains.

> How can we reward the people who keep staying? How can we recognize participation, trust, and long-term commitment?

The next category of finance will not compete on capital alone. It will compete on participation.

Capital moves, but communities stay. Liquidity changes, but reputation remains. And participation accumulates over time to become a powerful asset.

This shift creates a new opportunity. The opportunity to build a financial system that rewards **activity, reputation, identity, and long-term participation**.

RocX is designed for this future. A future where finance is no longer measured by TVL alone, but also by how many people keep participating.

We believe Survival Finance will become an essential layer of the on-chain economy.

Because the future of finance is not simply about money.

The finance of the future is about people. Those who explore. Those who contribute. Those who stay. And these people will define the next category of finance.

This is the opportunity RocX is building.
