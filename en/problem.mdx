# 🔍 What's Missing in DeFi?

## What is DeFi still missing?

DeFi grew quickly.

TVL increased,

protocols multiplied,

and more capital

moved on-chain.

But one thing

remains unsolved.

People do not stay.

---

> Liquidity can be attracted.
>
> Participation must be earned.

<Info>
💡 The problem is not liquidity.

It is retention.
