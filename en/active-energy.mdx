# Active Energy

## Financial energy created by activity

Every activity

leaves a trace.

In RocX,

that trace becomes

Active Energy.

Active Energy

circulates user participation

inside the ecosystem.

---

<Info>
📘 **Definition**

Active Energy(AE) is

RocX's basic unit of activity

created through Proof of Activity.

AE is not just a point system.

It is ecosystem energy

for measuring,

accumulating,

and using activity.
</Info>

> Energy is not given.
>
> It is created through participation.

## 💡 Why energy, not points?

Points are rewards

that end when they are collected.

Energy is created,

accumulated,

used,

and created again.

RocX chose

`energy`

instead of

`points`

because user activity should keep circulating.

## 📊 Points vs Active Energy

| Points | Active Energy |
| --- | --- |
| 🎁 Reward | Result of activity |
| 📦 Accumulation | 🔄 Circulation |
| 🏷 Event-centered | 🌍 Ecosystem-centered |
| 💰 Spending | 🚀 Utility |
| One-time | Continuous |
