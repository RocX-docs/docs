# 🛡 Survival Mechanism

## Built for Long-Term Participation

Growing fast

matters.

But lasting long

matters more.

RocX designs finance

that prioritizes

continuous participation

over short-term growth.

---

<Info>

trust,

activity,

and the ecosystem cycle

continuing over time.
