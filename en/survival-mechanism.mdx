## Designed to protect value

Growth matters. But sustainable growth matters far more.

Financial markets move in cycles. Bull markets inspire optimism, and bear markets test conviction.

Many ecosystems are designed to grow quickly, but few are designed to survive.

We believe a healthy ecosystem must stay resilient through every market cycle. Not only in times of growth, but also in times of uncertainty.

This philosophy is reflected in the RocX Survival Mechanism.

The Survival Mechanism is designed to strengthen the long-term resilience of the RocX ecosystem. Instead of relying only on short-term incentives, RocX is built on sustainable participation, repeated activity, and long-term relationships.

Real value should not depend on market sentiment alone. It must be supported by participation, strengthened by trust, and sustained through meaningful activity.

Growth matters. But sustainability matters more.

<Note>
RocX does not optimize for short-term expansion. We optimize for long-term survival.
</Note>

Because Survival Finance was not made for a single cycle. Survival Finance was made for the people who endure.

The strongest ecosystem is not the one that grows the fastest. It is the one that survives.

And survival creates value.
