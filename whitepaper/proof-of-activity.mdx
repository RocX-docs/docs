# Proof of Activity

Proof of Activity is the starting point of RocX's activity layer.

It turns meaningful user actions into verified signals that can move through the RocX ecosystem.

The purpose is not to count every action.

The purpose is to recognize activity that can carry value.

<Info>
Activity becomes useful when it can be trusted.
</Info>

---

## Activity is not the same as proof.

A transaction can happen without creating lasting value.

A click can happen without meaningful participation.

Proof of Activity separates meaningful activity from noise.

It gives RocX a way to recognize activity that matters.

---

## Not every action has the same value.

RocX does not treat every action equally.

Activity becomes meaningful when it shows participation, contribution, or continued engagement.

Proof of Activity creates a common way to recognize that difference.

---

## It connects through one shared standard.

Web3 activity can happen across many ecosystems.

Proof of Activity connects those actions through one standard inside RocX.

This makes activity usable across Active Energy, Reputation, and DeFi Planet.

---

## Proof of Activity is the starting point.

Proof of Activity is not the final benefit.

It is the first step that lets activity move into the RocX experience.

From there, activity can become energy, trust, identity, and better financial opportunity.
