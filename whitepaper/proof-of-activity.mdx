# Proof of Activity

The Foundation of Web3 Activity

Proof of Activity is RocX's core system for turning meaningful Web3 activity into verifiable value.
We do not just record activity.
We recognize it, connect it to value, and prepare it for financial benefits inside RocX.

<Info>
**Activity becomes valuable only when it can be verified.**
</Info>

---

## Activity is not the same as proof.

Proof of Activity does not store activity as a passive log.
It turns meaningful action into a verifiable signal.

That signal can travel across RocX.
It can support benefits, reputation, and future product experiences.

---

## Not every action counts.

Proof of Activity does not recognize every click.
It focuses on activity that is verifiable, meaningful, and relevant to the ecosystem.

  <Card title="DeFi Participation" icon="coins">On-chain financial use can create a strong activity signal.</Card>
  <Card title="NFT Verification" icon="image">Ownership and community context can be verified as activity.</Card>
  <Card title="Community Engagement" icon="users">Participation and contribution can become part of the record.</Card>
  <Card title="Governance" icon="scale-balanced">Voting and protocol involvement show ongoing commitment.</Card>
  <Card title="Partner Campaigns" icon="hands-helping">Partner actions can be recognized when they are meaningful.</Card>
  <Card title="Web3 Identity" icon="id-card">Verified activity helps form a stronger user identity.</Card>
</CardGroup>

---

## One common standard.

Web3 is made of many ecosystems.
Their activities look different.

Proof of Activity brings those activities into one shared standard.
A user can move across ecosystems without losing the value of what they have already done.

---

## Proof of Activity is the starting point.

Proof of Activity is not the end goal.
Verified activity becomes Active Energy.
Active Energy supports Reputation.
Reputation and activity history then connect to DeFi Planet benefits.

Proof of Activity is the beginning of that chain.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Meaningful Activity" icon="sparkles">A user performs an action that matters to the ecosystem.</Step>
  <Step title="Verification" icon="badge-check">RocX confirms that the activity meets its criteria.</Step>
  <Step title="Proof of Activity" icon="fingerprint">The activity becomes a recognized proof signal.</Step>
  <Step title="Active Energy" icon="zap">The proof signal can be converted into AE.</Step>
  <Step title="Reputation" icon="badge-check">Repeated proof accumulates into lasting reputation.</Step>
  <Step title="DeFi Benefits" icon="gift">Reputation and AE can support better DeFi outcomes.</Step>
</Steps>
