# Proof of Activity Planet

Proof of Activity Planet is the engine for verifying diverse Web3 activity. Its purpose is to strengthen DeFi Planet benefits.

## PoA Planet Overview

PoA Planet verifies activity and connects verified activity to Active Energy boosts. Users can turn activity from different Web3 contexts into useful RocX signals.

## NFT & PFP Verification

NFT holder and PFP verification are early use cases. Community identity and participation context can connect to AE Boost and DeFi Planet benefits.

## Expandable Activity Domains

AI, RWA, L1/L2, ZK, and BTCFi are examples of expandable activity domains. RocX is designed to recognize meaningful Web3 activity beyond a single sector.
