## A space that connects activity

Web3 contains many forms of activity.

But each activity often remains separated inside its own ecosystem.

PoA Planet verifies those activities through one shared standard and connects them into RocX.

The purpose is not to collect activity for its own sake.

The purpose is to turn verified activity into value that can be used inside RocX.
