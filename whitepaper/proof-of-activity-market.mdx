# PoA Market

Web3 activity is growing across ecosystems.

RocX focuses on the market opportunity created by connecting that activity to financial value.

---

## More Web3 Activity

Users now participate across communities, protocols, campaigns, NFTs, DeFi, AI, RWA, L1s, L2s, and more.

Activity is becoming one of the largest signals in Web3.

---

## Missing Infrastructure

Most activity remains fragmented.

There is still a need for infrastructure that verifies activity and makes it useful across product experiences.

---

## RocX Starting Point

RocX starts by connecting activity to DeFi Planet.

This gives activity a direct path into financial experience.

---

## Market Expansion

As Web3 activity expands, the need for activity verification can expand with it.

PoA can become a broader activity layer across ecosystems.

---

## RocX Opportunity

RocX can become infrastructure for turning Web3 activity into financial opportunity.

That opportunity grows as more ecosystems need meaningful activity signals.
