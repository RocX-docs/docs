# Proof of Activity Market

The Proof of Activity Market starts from the idea that Web3 activity can become a new value standard. RocX expands this market from DeFi.

## More Web3 Activity

Web3 is producing more activity across chains, apps, communities, campaigns, and verification layers. Users leave valuable signals across many contexts.

## Missing Infrastructure

There is still limited infrastructure for recognizing this activity across contexts and connecting it to financial benefits. Many activity records remain trapped inside individual platforms.

## RocX Starting Point

RocX starts with DeFi Planet because it is where users can directly experience benefits. Proof of Activity becomes valuable when verified activity strengthens real financial outcomes.

## Market Expansion

A PoA-based activity market can expand across NFT, DeFi, AI, RWA, L1/L2, ZK, BTCFi, and community sectors. The key is not the sector list, but the shared structure for verified activity.

## RocX Opportunity

RocX can become infrastructure for connecting activity to DeFi benefits. **Every Web3 Activity Creates Value** summarizes this market direction.
