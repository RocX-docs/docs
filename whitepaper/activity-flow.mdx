## From action to benefit

<Steps>
  <Step title="Activity happens">
    Users create activity through financial usage, partner campaigns, community participation, NFT verification, governance, or broader Web3 ecosystems.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Activity is verified">
    RocX checks whether the activity is meaningful, relevant, reliable, and connected to the ecosystem.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Active Energy grows">
    Verified activity contributes to Active Energy and becomes part of the user's activity profile.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reputation accumulates">
    Repeated participation creates a longer-term signal of trust, consistency, and intent.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Benefits become available">
    Active Energy and reputation can connect to boosts, tiers, better conditions, and additional reward opportunities.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Benefits should reward quality.

RocX does not treat activity as a decorative score or a simple volume game.

A better activity system should consider quality, consistency, relevance, and reliability. This helps benefits move toward users who create real value for the ecosystem.
