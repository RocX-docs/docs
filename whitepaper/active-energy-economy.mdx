# Active Energy Economy

The Active Energy Economy explains how AE is created and used inside RocX. AE is an activity-based economic unit, not a simple point.

## AE Creation

AE is created from verified activity. DeFi participation, activity verification, missions, and campaigns can become AE inputs through Proof of Activity.

## AE Accumulation

Users accumulate AE through repeated activity. Accumulated AE becomes part of Activity Identity and Reputation.

## AE Boost

AE Boost strengthens the effect of specific verified activity. It is designed to improve benefits inside DeFi Planet.

## AE Utility

AE can connect to conditions, tiers, additional benefits, and opportunities in DeFi Planet. RocX treats AE as a link between activity value and financial experience.

## AE and Reputation

AE can flow into the Reputation Layer over time. Repeated meaningful activity creates a long-term trust record and can support broader financial opportunities.
