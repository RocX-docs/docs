# Active Energy Economy

Active Energy is not only earned.

It moves through the RocX economy as users participate, receive boosts, and access better experiences.

---

## AE Creation

Active Energy is created through meaningful user activity.

Financial activity, verified Web3 activity, and exploration can all contribute to creation.

---

## AE Accumulation

Users accumulate AE by continuing to participate.

Accumulation gives activity a record that can grow across the user journey.

---

## AE Boost

Boosts strengthen the value of verified activity.

They help connect PoA Planet and Explore Planet back into DeFi Planet.

---

## AE Utility

AE can support better product experiences, stronger benefits, and more opportunity inside RocX.

It is designed to move through the ecosystem rather than remain isolated.

---

## AE and Reputation

AE can flow into Reputation over time.

This connects activity value with a longer record of trust.
