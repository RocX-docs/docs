Web3 has activity everywhere.

Users join communities, verify identities, use protocols, participate in campaigns, and move across ecosystems.

But most of that activity does not connect to financial experience.

RocX starts from this gap.

---

## Activity is everywhere.

Users already create value through participation.

They test products, join missions, support communities, use protocols, and contribute to ecosystem growth.

The problem is not the absence of activity.

The problem is that activity often remains scattered.

---

## Activity becomes valuable when it connects.

Activity needs a structure that can verify, connect, and carry it into product experience.

When activity connects to Active Energy, Reputation, and DeFi Planet, it can become part of a user's financial journey.

Proof of Activity exists for that connection.
