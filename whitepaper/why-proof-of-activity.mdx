# Why Proof of Activity Matters

## Why Proof of Activity Matters

Web3 has proven ownership,  
transactions,  
and assets.

But user activity is still not recognized in a shared way.

<Info>
Ownership proves what you have.  
Proof of Activity proves what you've done.
</Info>

---

## Activity Is Everywhere

Every day in Web3, activity happens everywhere.

Holding NFTs, using a new L1, joining DAOs, testing AI projects, using DeFi, and growing communities all contribute to ecosystem growth.

But most of that activity ends up recorded only inside the project where it happened.

---

## Activity Does Not Connect

Most Web3 activity is still disconnected.

Participation in one project rarely carries into another.

The activity and trust users build over time often has to start over in every new ecosystem.

RocX is built to address that break.

---

## Proof of Activity

Proof of Activity turns meaningful Web3 activity into a verifiable signal.

Verified activity does not end as a record.

It becomes Active Energy,  
can accumulate into Reputation over time,  
and can support better benefits inside DeFi Planet.
