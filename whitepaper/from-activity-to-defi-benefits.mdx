From Activity to DeFi Benefits is the final connection point in the RocX structure.

It explains how verified activity can move through Active Energy and reputation, then become part of better conditions, tiers, and additional opportunities inside DeFi Planet.

<Info>
RocX starts with activity, but the destination is a better financial experience.
</Info>

---

## The value path

RocX connects activity to DeFi benefits through a simple path.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Activity happens">
    A user creates meaningful activity across DeFi, partner campaigns, communities, NFTs, governance, or broader Web3 ecosystems.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Activity is verified">
    RocX interprets the activity through verification and scoring criteria.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Activity becomes Active Energy">
    Verified activity can contribute to Active Energy and strengthen the user's activity profile.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reputation accumulates">
    Repeated participation becomes a longer-term signal of trust and intent.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Benefits become available">
    Active Energy and reputation can connect to better DeFi conditions, tiers, and reward opportunities.
  </Step>
</Steps>

---

## Why DeFi is the destination

RocX does not create activity value only for display.

The core idea is that meaningful activity should improve the user's financial path. DeFi Planet is where that value can become visible through products such as Deposit, Lending, Swap, and Bridge.

This keeps Proof of Activity connected to practical financial utility.

---

## Benefits can evolve over time.

The first benefits may be simple.

Users may receive boosts, campaign rewards, tier movement, or better participation opportunities. As the ecosystem matures, activity and reputation can become deeper parts of benefit logic.

The important point is that RocX creates the infrastructure for that evolution.

---

## A system for continued participation

A one-time reward can attract users, but continued value keeps users engaged.

By connecting activity to long-term DeFi benefits, RocX gives users a reason to keep building their activity history instead of treating each campaign as an isolated event.

<Info>
Activity creates Active Energy. Active Energy strengthens reputation. Reputation opens the path to better DeFi benefits.
</Info>
