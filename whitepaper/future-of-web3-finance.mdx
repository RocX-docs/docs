# The Future of Web3 Finance

The next stage of Web3 finance can reflect both capital and activity. RocX aims to build that flow around DeFi Planet.

## What DeFi Measured

DeFi measured capital, collateral, liquidity, and TVL. This remains a core foundation of Web3 finance and of RocX.

## What RocX Connects

RocX adds activity to that foundation. Proof of Activity connects meaningful Web3 activity to Active Energy and Reputation.

## Activity as Financial Value

Verified activity can lead to better benefits and opportunities inside DeFi Planet. Users can experience activity as a force that strengthens finance.

## Closing Message

RocX builds an activity-based finance layer around DeFi. **Every Web3 Activity Creates Value.**
