# The Future of Web3 Finance

## The Future of Web3 Finance

Web3 brought assets onchain.

It brought finance onchain.

What remains is activity.

RocX is building a new form of Web3 finance where every meaningful activity can become value.

<Info>
The future of finance  
belongs to those  
who continue to participate.
</Info>

---

## Finance keeps evolving.

Traditional finance grew around capital.

DeFi brought finance onchain.

RocX aims to connect user activity as part of finance.

---

## Activity is a new financial asset.

In the future of Web3, activity will not end as a record.

Activity becomes trust, opportunity, and part of the financial experience.

We believe this is the next step for Web3 finance.

---

## RocX's vision

RocX is not a project trying to create a new chain.

It is not a project trying to create a new token economy.

We are building new financial infrastructure where meaningful activity from anywhere in Web3 can be recognized within one value system.

---

## Activity now creates value.

Users are no longer evaluated only by assets.

The activity, participation, trust, and experience they build over time can also become value.

This is the future of Web3 finance RocX is building toward.
