Activity-Based Benefits connect verified activity to benefit criteria inside RocX.

RocX does not treat activity as a decorative score. Activity becomes useful when it can improve the user's financial experience, strengthen reputation, and create a clearer reason to keep participating.

<Info>
Activity-Based Benefits are the bridge between Proof of Activity and DeFi Planet.
</Info>

---

## Benefits begin with verified activity.

Every benefit should start from activity that RocX can understand.

That activity may come from DeFi usage, partner campaigns, community participation, NFT verification, governance, or broader Web3 ecosystem activity.

Once the activity is verified, RocX can connect it to Active Energy, reputation, and benefit criteria.

---

## Benefits should reflect quality, not only volume.

Activity is not valuable just because it happened many times.

RocX looks at whether activity is meaningful, repeatable, relevant to the ecosystem, and connected to long-term participation.

This allows RocX to avoid a simple volume game and instead build a structure where better activity can lead to better opportunity.

## The goal is better finance.

Activity-Based Benefits are not separate from RocX's financial direction.

They are designed to make DeFi more personal, more participatory, and more sustainable.

Users who create meaningful activity should be able to move toward a better financial experience.
