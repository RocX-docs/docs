Verification and scoring help RocX interpret activity through reliable criteria.

Scoring is not designed to rank users for its own sake. It exists to make activity understandable, comparable, and usable inside product experiences.

<Info>
Verification asks whether an activity can be trusted. Scoring asks how that activity should be interpreted.
</Info>

---

## Verification comes first.

Before an activity can influence Active Energy, reputation, or benefits, RocX needs to decide whether the signal is valid.

This includes checking the source, category, timing, duplication risk, and connection to supported activity types.

A verified signal gives RocX a cleaner foundation for user value.

---

## Scoring adds weight.

Not every verified activity should carry the same weight.

A repeated low-effort action, a meaningful DeFi interaction, a long-running campaign contribution, and a verified ecosystem role may all represent different levels of participation.

Scoring helps RocX reflect that difference.

---

## A practical scoring model

RocX can evaluate activity through several dimensions.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Relevance" icon="target">
    How closely the activity connects to RocX's supported ecosystem and financial direction.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Quality" icon="badge-check">
    Whether the activity represents meaningful participation instead of mechanical repetition.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Consistency" icon="repeat">
    Whether the user participates over time rather than only once.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Reliability" icon="shield-check">
    Whether the source and proof are credible enough to affect benefits.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

---

## Scoring should remain explainable.

A good activity system should not feel like a black box.

RocX should make the main logic clear: better activity, stronger verification, and more consistent participation can contribute to stronger Active Energy and reputation.

<Info>
The purpose of scoring is to connect activity to value in a fair, understandable way.
</Info>
