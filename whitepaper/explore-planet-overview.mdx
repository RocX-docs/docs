## Daily Proof of Activity

In Explore Planet, users can earn Proof of Activity through new activity every day.

Daily Proof of Activity is a daily participation program that helps users continue engaging.

Users can join different explorations each day and earn additional Active Energy.

Explore Planet is the participation layer that keeps users moving inside the RocX ecosystem.

## Activity flywheel

Explore Planet does not end with paying out Active Energy.

Every exploration is designed to grow with the ecosystem.

When a mission is completed, 10% of earned Active Energy returns to the ecosystem.

---

## Sustainable activity economy

Exploration missions are not only reward events.

A portion of earned Active Energy returns to the ecosystem.

Part is permanently removed to help maintain ecosystem balance.

The rest returns to the reward pool to support new activity and rewards for future participants.

Through this structure, Explore Planet connects continued participation with ecosystem growth.

The activity economy is a structure where participation leads to the next participation.

Explore Planet is where that flow begins.

---

## Exploration does not end.

Explore Planet is an expandable space where new activity can continue entering.

New missions, new partners, and new activity types can keep coming into Explore Planet.

Users can discover more activity, prove more activity, and accumulate more Active Energy.

That activity connects back to PoA Planet and DeFi Planet.
