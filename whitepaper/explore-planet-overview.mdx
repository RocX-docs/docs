# Explore Planet

Explore Planet is a supporting mission and campaign area. It helps users discover additional activity and maintain participation.

## Explore Planet Overview

Explore Planet does not replace DeFi Planet or Proof of Activity Planet. It widens activity opportunities and supports participation around the main DeFi Planet experience.

## Missions

Missions guide users toward specific actions. Completed missions can become AE inputs or boosts when they meet Proof of Activity criteria.

## Campaigns

Campaigns are partner, community, or ecosystem participation programs. They help users experience different Web3 contexts through RocX.

## Community Activity

Community activity can show continued participation and user context. It can become part of Activity Identity and Reputation.

## AE and Retention

Explore activity can support AE earning and retention. The core financial benefits remain centered in DeFi Planet, while Explore Planet supports the activity layer around it.
