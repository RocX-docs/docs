# RocX

## Every Web3 Activity Creates Value

RocX is a Web3 finance platform centered on **DeFi Planet**.  
Users begin with deposits and lending in DeFi Planet, then strengthen their **Active Energy** by verifying meaningful Web3 activity in **Proof of Activity Planet**.

Active Energy is the unit that turns activity into value inside RocX.  
It connects verified activity to better DeFi benefits and long-term **Reputation**.

---

## What is RocX?

RocX connects Web3 activity to financial benefits through **Proof of Activity**, with DeFi Planet as the main product experience.

Traditional DeFi has measured users through capital and TVL. RocX adds activity, verification, participation, and reputation to that financial context.

<Info>
DeFi is RocX's financial engine. Proof of Activity is the activity verification system that strengthens that engine.
</Info>

## Why DeFi Planet Stays at the Center

Proof of Activity is RocX's key differentiator, but the main RocX experience still begins in **DeFi Planet**.

Users deposit capital in DeFi Planet, verify activity in Proof of Activity Planet, and use Active Energy to access better benefits.

RocX is not building an activity score for its own sake. It is building a structure where activity can become real financial opportunity.

<Note>
Every Web3 activity can create value. DeFi Planet is where that value becomes financial opportunity.
</Note>
