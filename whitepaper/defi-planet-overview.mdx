## Finance starts here.

DeFi Planet is RocX's main planet.

Every financial activity starts from this space.

Users deposit assets, borrow, repay, and accumulate Active Energy over time.

In RocX, DeFi Planet is not only a place where features are collected.

It is the first space where activity turns into financial value.

---

## Financial activity is activity.

Depositing assets does not end with one transaction.

Inside RocX, the financial action itself becomes meaningful activity.

The more users continue using DeFi Planet, the more Active Energy they can accumulate.

What matters is not one action. It is participation that continues.

## Proof of Activity strengthens DeFi.

DeFi Planet does not stand alone.

Verified activity from PoA Planet connects to AE Boost.

As a result, users can receive more benefits inside DeFi Planet.

Proof of Activity does not replace DeFi.

It is a new layer that makes the financial experience stronger.

That leads back into a better financial experience.

DeFi Planet is the core product where that long-term flow begins.
