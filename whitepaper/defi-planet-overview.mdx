# DeFi Planet

DeFi Planet is the main RocX product. Users perform financial actions here and can experience stronger benefits through Active Energy and activity history.

## DeFi Planet Overview

DeFi Planet is the central financial engine of RocX. Proof of Activity Planet and Explore Planet connect back to DeFi Planet to strengthen the user experience.

## Deposit

Deposit is the basic entry point into the RocX financial experience. Users participate in DeFi Planet with assets and can later connect that participation to activity-based benefits.

## Lending

Lending is a core financial function of DeFi Planet. RocX adds activity-based signals to make this financial experience more contextual.

## Borrowing / Repay

Borrowing and repayment complete the user financial flow. RocX can reflect repayment and activity history as part of a broader trust and benefit structure.

## Activity-Based Benefits

Verified activity and AE strengthen DeFi Planet benefits. AE Boost can connect to better conditions, tiers, and additional opportunities.
