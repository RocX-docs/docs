# Active Energy

Active Energy is the value that accumulates from user activity inside RocX.

It helps connect activity with product experience across the ecosystem.

When users continue participating, their activity can keep moving forward instead of disappearing after one interaction.

<Info>
Activity should not disappear.

It should accumulate.
</Info>

---

## RocX runs on a shared unit.

RocX connects different forms of activity across DeFi Planet, PoA Planet, and Explore Planet.

Active Energy gives those activities a shared unit of value.

It helps RocX connect separate user actions into one experience.

---

## Active Energy comes from three planets.

Users can accumulate Active Energy through financial activity in DeFi Planet.

They can strengthen it through verified activity in PoA Planet.

They can also earn more through missions and participation in Explore Planet.

---

## Active Energy moves forward.

Active Energy is designed to support the next action.

It can connect to boosts, benefits, reputation, and more participation.

The value created by activity continues through the RocX experience.

---

## More activity, more value.

As users participate more, Active Energy can grow.

That value can lead to more benefits.

It can also help create a stronger record of trust over time.
