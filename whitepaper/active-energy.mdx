The shared value unit that moves RocX.

Active Energy is how user activity accumulates inside the RocX ecosystem.

Activity does not disappear as a single record.
It becomes value that can continue into the next experience.

<Info>
**Activity accumulates.**

**Accumulated activity leads to better opportunities.**
</Info>

---

## RocX runs on one shared unit.

Different activities are connected into one experience inside RocX.

Active Energy gives those activities a shared unit of value.

The longer a user participates in RocX,
the more their activity can become lasting value.

---

## Active Energy moves into the next experience.

Active Energy does not stop at accumulation.

It can lead to better benefits, deeper reputation, and more participation.

As activity continues, the financial experience inside RocX can expand with it.

---

## More participation creates more value.

RocX values continued participation over accidental clicks.

Active Energy is designed so that user time, participation, and contribution
can be recognized inside the ecosystem.
