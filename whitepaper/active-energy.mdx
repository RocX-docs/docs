# Active Energy

The Energy That Powers RocX

Active Energy is RocX's common unit for meaningful activity.
It is not a point system or a reward balance.
It is the value that activity creates across the RocX ecosystem.

<Info>
**Activity creates energy.**

**Energy creates opportunity.**
</Info>

---

## RocX runs on a shared unit.

Active Energy connects activity across RocX.
It is the common unit that turns participation into a value RocX can carry forward.

As AE accumulates, it supports better benefits, stronger reputation, and more room for future participation.

---

## Active Energy comes from three planets.

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="DeFi Planet" icon="coins">The main source of AE. Deposit, lending, and repayment build Active Energy over time. This is RocX's core financial engine.</Card>
  <Card title="Proof of Activity Planet" icon="fingerprint">This planet verifies Web3 activity. Verified activity adds AE and boosts the value of DeFi Planet. It strengthens DeFi rather than replacing it.</Card>
  <Card title="Explore Planet" icon="compass">Missions, campaigns, and community participation can generate additional AE. It supports ongoing participation across the ecosystem.</Card>
</CardGroup>

---

## Active Energy moves forward.

AE is not meant to stay still.
It accumulates through participation, and that accumulation moves into better financial experiences.

<Steps>
  <Step title="DeFi Activity" icon="coins">Core financial activity builds the base layer of AE.</Step>
  <Step title="Proof of Activity" icon="fingerprint">Verified activity adds strength and boosts.</Step>
  <Step title="Explore Activity" icon="compass">Missions and campaigns add extra participation value.</Step>
  <Step title="Active Energy" icon="zap">All three sources accumulate into one common unit.</Step>
  <Step title="DeFi Benefits" icon="gift">AE can help unlock better conditions and opportunities.</Step>
  <Step title="Reputation" icon="badge-check">Repeated activity turns AE into long-term trust.</Step>
</Steps>

---

## More activity, more value.

Active Energy is not designed to reward a single action.
It is designed to connect sustained participation with long-term value.

The longer a user stays active in RocX,
the more AE they can accumulate,
and the more meaningful their future experience becomes.
