# Reputation Layer

Trust accumulated through activity

Not every activity carries the same meaning.

Users who keep participating, continue contributing, and build trust inside the ecosystem need value that reflects that record.

The Reputation Layer is the structure that accumulates that trust.

<Info>
Trust is not claimed.

It is accumulated.
</Info>

---

## Activity becomes trust.

Proof of Activity verifies activity.

Active Energy accumulates the value of that activity.

Reputation is trust created from accumulated activity over time.

RocX believes that a user's past activity can lead to future opportunity.

---

## Reputation is not created in a day.

Reputation is not created by one event, one transaction, or one activity.

Consistent participation, continued activity, and records built over time create reputation.

RocX treats long-term participation as one of the most important forms of value.

---

## Trust creates more opportunity.

Reputation does not only represent a higher score.

It becomes a basis for better financial experience, more opportunity, and lasting trust.

Reputation is the layer that connects Active Energy and user activity to practical value.

---

## Reputation is RocX's long-term value.

Finance is not built only from assets.

Trust is also an asset.

RocX accumulates user activity over time and connects the result to a new value called Reputation.

This record of trust becomes the basis for explaining how user activity becomes identity in the next step.

---

<Info>
Reputation is not an instant reward.  
It is earned through continued participation.
</Info>

Long-term activity creates long-term trust.
