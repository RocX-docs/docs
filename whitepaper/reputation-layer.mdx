# Reputation Layer

The Reputation Layer is where user activity and Active Energy accumulate into long-term trust records. RocX is designed so activity can become the basis for ongoing benefits.

## From AE to Reputation

AE is the unit of verified activity, while Reputation is the record of that activity over time. As user context accumulates, it can create richer financial signals.

## A Record of Trust

Reputation can show what a user has done and how consistently they have participated. This context is difficult to express through wallet balance alone.

## Relationship to DeFi Benefits

Reputation can connect to tiers, conditions, and opportunities in DeFi Planet. RocX aims to make activity-based trust part of the financial experience.

<Info>
The Reputation Layer complements DeFi Planet. RocX is building a financial experience where capital and activity can both matter.
</Info>

## Long-Term Meaning

Users build their own trust record through repeated activity. That record can become a basis for broader financial opportunities.
