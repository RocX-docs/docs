A user's activity becomes a new form of identity.

Wallet addresses can show where assets are held.

They do not fully show what a user has done, how they have participated, or how trust has grown over time.

Activity Identity connects those records into a user-level signal.

<Info>
Identity is not only an address.

It is also what the user has built through activity.
</Info>

---

## A wallet address is not enough.

In Web3, wallet addresses are often treated as user identity.

But an address alone cannot explain participation.

It cannot show contribution, consistency, or the context behind user behavior.

RocX sees activity as a more complete signal.

---

## Activity creates identity.

When activity is verified and accumulated, it becomes more than a transaction record.

It becomes a signal that shows how a user participates inside the ecosystem.

Activity Identity is created from repeated participation, verified behavior, and accumulated value.

---

## Activity Identity is not personal information.

Activity Identity does not require private personal data.

It is based on activity signals that can be verified inside Web3.

The goal is not to reveal who a user is offline.

The goal is to show what the user has built through participation.

---

## Activity Identity leads to better opportunity.

A stronger activity identity can support better experiences across RocX.

It can connect to Reputation, Active Energy, and DeFi Planet benefits.

This is how user activity becomes a foundation for future opportunity.
