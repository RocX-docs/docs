# AE Boosting System

The AE Boosting System connects activity signals to stronger DeFi Planet benefits. Boosts are not the final goal; they are mechanisms for better financial experience.

## NFT/PFP Verification Boost

NFT holder and PFP verification can express community context. These signals can connect to AE Boost and strengthen DeFi Planet benefits.

## Web3 Activity Verification Boost

Diverse Web3 activity can be verified through Proof of Activity. Verified activity creates user context and can become a basis for AE Boost.

## DeFi Activity Boost

Financial activity inside DeFi Planet is also an important boost input. RocX can combine DeFi activity and external Web3 activity for richer benefits.

## Mission and Campaign Boost

Explore Planet missions and campaigns encourage additional participation. Completed participation can connect to AE earning or boosts.

## Long-Term Participation Boost

Consistent participation can become a stronger trust signal than one-time activity. RocX is designed so long-term activity can support Reputation and DeFi benefits.
