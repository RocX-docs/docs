## Recognizing Meaningful Participation

Not every activity carries the same meaning.

RocX recognizes verified activity, continued participation, and ecosystem contribution with greater value.

AE Boost is the system that reflects that difference.

<Info>
Not every activity is equal.  
Meaningful activity deserves greater recognition.
</Info>

---

## Why does Boost exist?

Every activity can create Active Energy.

But activity that creates higher trust and contributes more to the ecosystem needs to be recognized with higher value.

AE Boost exists to reflect that difference.

---

## How is Boost created?

AE Boost is based on verifiable activity.

The more meaningful, consistent, and positive a user's activity is for the ecosystem, the stronger the Boost can become.

This page does not explain the calculation method.

---

## Boost strengthens the financial experience.

AE Boost is not only a way to distribute more Active Energy.

Higher Active Energy can lead to a better financial experience in DeFi Planet.

The purpose of Boost is to connect long-term participation to greater value.

---

## A system that keeps expanding

AE Boost is not limited to a fixed set of activities.

As the RocX ecosystem expands, new activity can become eligible for Boost.

What matters is not which project it comes from, but how meaningful the activity is.
