# AE Boosting System

AE Boost is how RocX strengthens activity value.

Boosts help verified activity become more meaningful inside DeFi Planet and across the user journey.

---

## NFT/PFP Verification Boost

Users can receive boosts by verifying NFT ownership, PFP identity, or collection-based participation.

These signals help connect community identity with activity value.

---

## Web3 Activity Verification Boost

Meaningful Web3 activity can strengthen Active Energy when it is verified.

This includes activity across partner ecosystems, protocols, campaigns, and communities.

---

## DeFi Activity Boost

Financial actions inside DeFi Planet can also support boosts.

Deposits, borrowing, repayment, and continued financial participation can strengthen the user's activity record.

---

## Mission and Campaign Boost

Explore missions and ecosystem campaigns can create additional boost opportunities.

This lets users keep discovering new ways to participate.

---

## Long-Term Participation Boost

Consistent participation matters more than isolated activity.

RocX can use boost structures to recognize users who keep contributing over time.
