# RocX

### Survival Finance powered by Proof of Activity

> Activity should not disappear.
>

---

## What RocX is

RocX is an on-chain financial layer built around **Proof of Activity**.

Instead of measuring users only by the capital they bring in, RocX records how they participate, how consistently they stay, and what kind of trust they build over time.

<Info>
RocX turns meaningful participation into financial signals through Active Energy, reputation, and identity.
</Info>

## Why it matters

Most DeFi systems start from capital.

RocX starts from activity.

It is designed for users who do more than deposit assets. They explore, contribute, return, and build a history that should matter inside finance.

## DeFi and RocX

| Traditional DeFi | RocX |
| --- | --- |
| Capital-first | Activity-first |
| TVL as the main signal | Reputation as a financial signal |

</Tip>
