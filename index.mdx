## Every Web3 Activity Creates Value

RocX is a Web3 finance platform centered on **DeFi**.  
Users can begin with core financial activity, have meaningful Web3 activity recognized, and move toward a better financial experience.

Active Energy is the unit that turns activity into value inside RocX.  
It connects activity to better DeFi benefits and long-term reputation.

---

## What is RocX?

RocX connects Web3 activity to financial benefits through **Proof of Activity**, with DeFi as the main financial experience.

Traditional DeFi has measured users through capital and TVL. RocX adds activity, verification, participation, and reputation to that financial context.

<Info>
DeFi is RocX's financial engine. Proof of Activity is the activity verification system that strengthens that engine.
</Info>

## Why DeFi Stays at the Center

The main RocX experience begins in **DeFi**.

Users can build capital and activity together, then move toward a better financial experience.

RocX is not building an activity score for its own sake. It is building a structure where activity can become real financial opportunity.

<Note>
Every Web3 activity can create value. DeFi is where that value connects to the financial experience.
</Note>
