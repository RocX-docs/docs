# A new value flywheel

Traditional DeFi proved that capital can grow on-chain.

RocX builds on that foundation and adds another layer: participation, reputation, and retention.

The result is a value flywheel where users do not only bring liquidity. They help the ecosystem grow by staying active and building trust over time.

> Capital can start a protocol.
>
> Participation helps it survive.

---

## What RocX adds to DeFi

RocX does not try to discard the TVL-centered growth model.

It adds a new value cycle on top of it, where participation becomes measurable, reputation grows over time, and retention becomes part of the ecosystem's strength.

<Info>
The RocX Value Flywheel connects Participation, Proof of Activity, Active Energy, Reputation, Retention, and Ecosystem Growth into one repeating cycle.
</Info>

## Traditional DeFi and RocX

| Traditional DeFi | RocX |
| --- | --- |
| Capital first | Participation first |
| TVL growth | Retention growth |
| Incentive-driven activity | Meaningful activity |
| Transaction history | Reputation history |
| Liquidity | Community |
| Growth | Sustainable growth |

<Tip>
TVL still matters. RocX adds the missing question: who keeps participating after the capital arrives?
</Tip>

## The RocX perspective

RocX does not argue that TVL is unimportant.

It argues that TVL alone cannot explain whether finance is healthy.

Good finance should accumulate more than capital. It should also accumulate participation, trust, and reasons to return.

<Note>
TVL is an outcome.

Participation is the starting point.
</Note>
