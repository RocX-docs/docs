Every meaningful activity leaves a trace.

In RocX, that trace becomes **Active Energy**.

Active Energy, or AE, is the financial fuel that keeps participation moving through the ecosystem.

> Energy is not handed out.
>
> It is created through participation.

---

## What Active Energy is

Active Energy is the core unit of activity created through Proof of Activity.

It is generated when participation is verified, then used as a signal for reputation, identity, and future opportunity.

<Info>
AE helps RocX measure participation in a way that can circulate through the ecosystem instead of disappearing after one action.
</Info>

## Why energy, not points

Points usually work like rewards.

They are collected, counted, and often spent or forgotten.

Energy works differently. It is created through activity, accumulates through continued participation, and can support more activity later.

That is why RocX uses the idea of energy. Participation should keep moving.

---

## Points and Active Energy

| Points | Active Energy |
| --- | --- |
| Reward-centered | Activity-centered |
| Collected as a balance | Circulates through the ecosystem |
| Often tied to events | Connected to long-term participation |
| Usually spent or redeemed | Used as financial context |
| One-time value | Continuing signal |

<Tip>
Active Energy is not designed to sit still. It is designed to make participation visible, useful, and repeatable.
</Tip>
