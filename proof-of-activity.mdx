# How activity becomes value

Traditional finance records the movement of money.

RocX records the activity behind the user.

Proof of Activity is the mechanism that turns meaningful participation into financial data that can be measured, verified, and used across the RocX ecosystem.

> Every meaningful action deserves to be remembered.

---

## What Proof of Activity is

Proof of Activity, or PoA, is RocX's core mechanism for recording on-chain participation.

PoA connects user activity to Active Energy, reputation, identity, and future access. The goal is to make participation durable enough to matter inside finance.

<Info>
PoA gives RocX a way to recognize more than deposits and transactions. It helps the ecosystem understand how a user participates over time.
</Info>

## Why activity needs proof

In many DeFi systems, activity disappears when the incentive ends.

A user can explore, contribute, and return for months, but much of that history is not carried into the next financial opportunity.

PoA changes that relationship.

Activity becomes a record. Records become reputation. Reputation can support better access.

---

## Traditional DeFi and Proof of Activity

| Traditional DeFi | Proof of Activity |
| --- | --- |
| Deposit records | Activity records |
| TVL as the main signal | Participation as a financial signal |
| Collateral-based access | Reputation-based access |
| Transactions as history | Contribution as history |
| Past data stays isolated | Activity can shape future opportunity |

<Tip>
Proof of Activity does not replace capital data. It adds participation data, which helps finance understand the user behind the wallet.
</Tip>
