# 📈 Market Opportunity

## The Next Financial Category

Finance has evolved

whenever new assets emerged.

Now,

it is not a new asset

but new data

that will change finance.

RocX proposes

the next financial category

based on the data of

`activity`.

---

<Info>
📘 **Definition**

Survival Finance

does not replace

capital-centered finance.

It expands finance

by turning participation and trust

into financial data.
</Info>

> The next financial primitive won't measure more capital.
>
> It will measure more participation.

## 💡 Why now?

Web3 has created

more chains,

more protocols,

and more liquidity.

But structures that use

user activity,

contribution,

and trust

as financial data

are still early.

Participation is still

one of the most underestimated

on-chain assets.

## 📈 Financial Evolution

<Steps>
  <Step title="Traditional Finance" icon="landmark">
    Finance evaluated people and institutions through **Credit**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Digital Finance" icon="credit-card">
    Digital finance recorded user behavior through **Transactions**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="DeFi" icon="globe">
    DeFi made **Capital** the central signal of on-chain finance.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Survival Finance" icon="rocket">
    Survival Finance expands **Participation** into financial data.
  </Step>
</Steps>
