# The next financial category

Finance changes when a new kind of value becomes measurable.

Traditional finance measured credit. Digital finance measured transactions. DeFi made capital programmable on-chain.

The next shift is not only about a new asset. It is about a new kind of data: participation.

RocX proposes Survival Finance as a financial category built around that data.

> The next financial primitive will not only measure more capital.
>
> It will measure more participation.

---

## What Survival Finance changes

Survival Finance expands capital-centered finance by turning participation, contribution, and trust into financial context.

It gives protocols a way to understand not only what users hold, but how they participate and what trust they build over time.

<Info>
RocX treats activity as a financial signal. When activity is verified and accumulated, it can support reputation, identity, retention, and future access.
</Info>

## Why now

Web3 has created more chains, more protocols, and more liquidity.

But the structures that use activity, contribution, and trust as financial data are still early.

Participation remains one of the most underestimated on-chain assets.
