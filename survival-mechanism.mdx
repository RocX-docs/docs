## What the Survival Mechanism is

The Survival Mechanism is RocX's framework for keeping the ecosystem active over time.

It connects participation, retention, trust, and opportunity into a cycle that can continue after the first transaction.

<Info>
RocX does not measure sustainability by TVL alone. It also looks at whether users keep participating, whether trust grows, and whether the ecosystem gives people a reason to return.
</Info>

## What RocX wants to sustain

RocX is built to sustain more than liquidity.

It is built to sustain the behavior that makes a financial ecosystem durable.

## The RocX perspective

RocX does not define success by how many users arrive once.

It defines success by whether users have a reason to come back and keep building.

Sustainability does not come from rewards alone. It comes from an experience that keeps participation meaningful.

<Note>
Growth is not enough.

Survival requires retention, trust, and a reason to return.
</Note>
